# Learned Preferences: writing-voice

Corrections and preferences that persist across sessions. Manage via `/cdf:learn show writing-voice`, `/cdf:learn remove writing-voice {number}`.

## Do
- Use parenthetical asides for context, not dashes (2026-03-28)
- Name specific tools (TeamCreate, AgentTeams) over generic descriptions (2026-03-28)
- Use cross-domain metaphors from ML naturally, this is the strongest mode (2026-03-28)
- Use cost/benefit framing for qualitative claims ("the additional tokens spent are worth it") (2026-03-28)
- Use practical, specific framing ("caught blind spots", "found optimizations earlier in the flow") (2026-03-28)

## Don't
- Never use hyphens, em dashes, or en dashes. Use commas, periods, or parentheses instead. (2026-03-28)
- No manufactured punchline closers designed for impact over genuine observation (2026-03-28)

## Style
- "More often than not" is a natural conversational qualifier, not clutter (2026-03-28)
- Explain the WHY behind tool/approach choices ("shared context" as differentiator) (2026-03-28)

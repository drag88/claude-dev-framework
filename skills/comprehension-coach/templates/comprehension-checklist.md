# Comprehension Checklist — <session / change topic>

> Updated by the Comprehension Coach. An item is ticked **only** after verified mastery:
> the learner restated it, answered "why" at more than one level, handled a curveball,
> and (for solution/impact items) predicted what it affects.

## 1. The Problem  *(weight this heaviest)*
- [ ] What the problem was (concrete symptom, not vibe)
- [ ] **Why** the problem existed — root cause, drilled past the first answer
- [ ] The branches: what approaches were possible/considered
- [ ] Why the road not taken was rejected

## 2. The Solution
- [ ] What was actually done
- [ ] **Why** it was resolved this way (the design decision behind it)
- [ ] Key design tradeoffs made
- [ ] Edge cases it handles — and any it deliberately doesn't

## 3. The Broader Context
- [ ] **Why this matters** (the stakes)
- [ ] What the change impacts downstream
- [ ] What it unblocks, or what new risk it introduces

## Final synthesis
- [ ] Learner connects problem → solution → impact in one narrative, in their own words
